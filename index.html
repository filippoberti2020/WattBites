Pro Nutrition Tips

Evidence-based strategies from WorldTour team nutritionists.

The 90g Carb/Hour Standard

Top pros now consume 90g of carbs per hour during races (60g glucose + 30g fructose) to maximize absorption. This requires training your gut with progressively increasing carb intake during long rides.

The 30-Minute Recovery Window

Consume 1-1.2g carbs/kg and 0.3-0.4g protein/kg within 30 minutes post-ride to maximize glycogen resynthesis and muscle repair. Liquid forms are absorbed fastest.

Pre-Ride Carb Loading

For rides over 90 minutes, consume 3g carbs/kg body weight 3 hours before. Choose low-fiber options like white rice or oatmeal to avoid GI distress.

Hydration Strategy

Weigh yourself before and after rides. For every kg lost, drink 1.5L of fluid with electrolytes. Add 400-800mg sodium per liter in hot conditions.

Anti-Inflammatory Foods

After hard efforts, include foods high in omega-3s (salmon, walnuts), antioxidants (berries, tart cherry), and polyphenols (dark chocolate, green tea) to reduce inflammation.
